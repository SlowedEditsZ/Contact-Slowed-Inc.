## <font color="#FFA500">2. Nitrous (The 0.3s Window) 🚀</font>
<font size="5">**Timing is EVERYTHING.**</font>
When the race starts (exactly **3 seconds** in), you have a tiny window of **0.3s** to hit the Nitrous. 
* **Success:** Massive speed boost!
* **Fail:** You lose your momentum and the race.
---
## <font color="#00FF00">3. Fuel Management (The 250 Limit) ⛽</font>
Don't let your tank run dry!
* **Critical Limit:** <font color="#FF4500">**250 Units**</font>
* **Penalty:** If your fuel drops below 250, your car will slow down to a crawl.
* **Strategy:** Manage your acceleration to keep that needle above the danger zone.
